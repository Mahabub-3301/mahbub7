About Us

Food Waste Management is a platform dedicated to reducing food waste by connecting restaurants, charities, and customers. Our mission is to ensure that surplus food reaches those in need while promoting sustainability.

With our easy-to-use platform, restaurants can post surplus food, charities can claim donations, and customers can participate in reducing food waste. Together, we can make a difference!

Contact Us

If you have any questions or would like to collaborate, feel free to reach out to us at info@foodwastemanagement.com.
